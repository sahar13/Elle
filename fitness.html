Weekend workshops.

Super sexy. Slightly ridiculous. Amazing choreography.

Oh, and all with a licensed bar.

Body Like Beyonce

Want a body like Beyoncé? Don’t dream it, work hard and grind 'til you own it! In this intense 2 hour workshop you’ll be put through a killer muscle conditioning training, a full body stretching session followed by cardio derived from Beyoncé choreo, 16 counts from each of Beyoncé’s hottest videos.

Biebography Workshop

Our Bieboraphy choreo class allows you and your friends to learn the step-by-step moves to your favourite Bieber song, Sorry! So come Shake it, Twerk it, Work it – No dance experience necessary!

Coyote Ugly Workshop

The ultimate guilty pleasure – well, maybe there are a few other really great guilty pleasures but we digress! You've seen the movie and you know you want to try. You will learn a smokin' hot routine you can perform at your local watering hole. And don't think you're getting outta this class without actually doing a live performance on our own bar which we've specially designed with Coyote Ugly in mind!

Chair Dance Workshop

You'll never look at a simple kitchen chair the same way again! This class introduces you to the art of the chair dance while giving you the best abdominal workout you've ever had. We start with the basics and work our way through a routine so that at the end, you’ll be able to show off an unbelievable Chair Dance routine.
